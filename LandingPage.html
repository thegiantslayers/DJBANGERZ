411 on DJ BANGERZ

DJ BANGERZ love for music and technology have been life long passions. His love for music intersects with his love for technology through his application of his Electrical Engineering background. He earned a Bachelor's degree of Electrical Engineering from the University of Cincinnati and leverages his engineering  background in innovative music production that pushes the boundries of DJ-ing.

It all began in his home in Columbus when he was 5 years old and he took his first piano lessons. While he took piano lessons, he often took breaks to  tinker with appliances in his home and engineer them in new ways.

Those lessons created a strong foundation in music that was reinforced through his participation and leadership in the high school band. While in the high school band, he played in several groups, as the first chair and second chair. Although he played several instruments with great skill, he was master of the alto saxophone and loved its deep, resontating sounds.

These experiences shaped his music interest and later propelled him into the DJ scene. He was hired for his first DJ gig in the summer of 2015. From that time, he has enjoyed a successful DJ career that has allowed him to provide bangin' music to a wide range of clients, with just as varying venues, from Skully's Music Diner in Columbus,  Collegiate 100 Gala at the Unversity of Cincinnati, to the Jewish Young Professional Holiday Gathering .
